Welcome to Path Finding Visualizer

  • Click & Drag on the grid cells to make walls OR click on GENERATE MAZE to generate a maze.
  • Click & Drag to change the Start and End positions as you like.

Don't forget to select the Path Finding Algorithm before you click on VISUALIZE to get started.

Additionally, you can also,

  • Adjust the animation speed.
  • Click on ADD WEIGHT to populate the grid with random weights.
  • Drag Start and End positions to instantly generate a new path, even after the visualization is completed.
  • Click on CLEAR PATH or CLEAR MAZE to do what their name suggests.
