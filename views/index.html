Welcome to Path Finding Visulaizer

  • You can add walls by clicking on the grid cell.
  • You can also generate mazes and patterns by selecting them from the "MAZE ALGORITHMS" drop-down menu.
  • You can click and drag start and end points to move them inside the grid.
  • You can drag start and end points even after the algorithm visulaization is completed. This will allow you to see new path instantly.
  • You can click on "ADD WEIGHT" button to add random weights.
  • To clear path after visualization you can click on "CLEAR PATH" button.
  • To clear the whole maze you can click on "CLEAR MAZE" button.
  • You can select different algorithm from the "ALGORITHM" drop-down menu.
  • To change spped of the aimation slide speed bar to left and to increase do opposite.
  • And after all this to see the real magic click on "VISULIZE" button to start visualization.
